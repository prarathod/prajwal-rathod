ABOUT ME

I discovered web development in an interesting way. I was in Nagpur to give an exam and found it really hard to find a good mess and accommodation. When I returned home, I built a website to help students affordable rooms and messes based on peer reviews. It was then that I discovered my passion for coding and creating tech platforms that solve real-world hurdles.

I am a full-stack web developer with experience with Node.js, Express, React, Redux and MongoDB. Along with these, I excel at coding with languages like Python, and Java. Currently, I am training with Masai School to further boost my knowledge and skills.

Toolkit: React, Redux, Node, Express, Javascript, TailwindCSS, Bootstrap.

There's nothing I truly enjoy more than coding. But, when I do find some time, I also like traveling and fishing.
